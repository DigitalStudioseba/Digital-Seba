📜 Birth Registration Guide

⏰ Register within 45 days of birth for free. After 45 days, a fee applies.

Required Documents

  • ✅ Hospital birth record/discharge certificate
  • ✅ Father's NID card
  • ✅ Mother's NID card
  • ✅ Parents' marriage certificate (if available)
  • ✅ Hospital immunization card

Where to Register

  • 📍 Union Parishad office (rural)
  • 📍 Pourashava/City Corporation (urban)
  • 🌐 Online: bdris.gov.bd
  • 📞 Helpline: 16768

💀 Death Certificate Registration

Death must be registered within 45 days at the relevant local government office.

Required Documents

  • ✅ Hospital death certificate / doctor's certificate
  • ✅ Deceased person's NID card
  • ✅ Applicant's NID card
  • ✅ Relationship proof

Process

  1. Collect medical death certificate
  2. Visit local Union Parishad/Pourashava
  3. Fill death registration form
  4. Submit required documents
  5. Receive death certificate
