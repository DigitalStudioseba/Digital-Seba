🔄 Land Mutation (নামজারি)

⚠️ Mutation must be done after every land transfer/purchase to update ownership records.

Required Documents

  • ✅ Purchase deed (registered)
  • ✅ Seller's NID card
  • ✅ Buyer's NID card
  • ✅ Last paid tax receipt (Khajna)
  • ✅ Khatian copy (certified)
  • ✅ Dag/Plot map
  • ✅ Application form

Mutation Process

1
Apply at Upazila Land Office with documents
2
Pay Mutation fee (৳ varies by land value)
3
Field verification by Land Office staff
4
Mutation order issued (30-90 days)
5
Collect mutation khatian from Land Office
