📖 Passport Application Guide

Machine Readable Passport (MRP)

MRP passports are being phased out. New applications get ePassport by default.
  • ✅ Valid for 5 or 10 years
  • ✅ 48 pages available
  • ✅ Machine readable technology
  • ✅ Available at all Regional Passport Offices

Electronic Passport (ePassport)

  • ✅ RFID chip with biometric data
  • ✅ More secure than MRP
  • ✅ Faster immigration processing
  • ✅ Available in 48 or 64 pages
  • ✅ Online application at epassport.gov.bd

🔄 Passport Renewal

⏰ Important: Renew your passport at least 6 months before expiry to avoid travel disruptions.

Renewal Documents

  • ✅ Current/expired passport (original)
  • ✅ National ID card
  • ✅ Passport size photo (latest)
  • ✅ Fee payment receipt
  • ✅ Completed application form

Renewal Process

  1. Apply online at epassport.gov.bd
  2. Select "Renewal" application type
  3. Upload existing passport details
  4. Pay the applicable fee
  5. Visit the passport office with original documents
  6. Collect new passport when ready
